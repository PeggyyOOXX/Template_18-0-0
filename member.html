VIP Daily Rebate Policy

1. Rebate Time: The system automatically distributes the previous day’s valid betting rebate between 12:00 and 18:00 daily. Rebate funds can be withdrawn with just 1x turnover. The daily rebate cap is NT$600,000.

2. Excluded Bets: Bets placed on Super Lotto, virtual currency games, and fishing games are not counted toward valid bets, rebate calculation, or withdrawal conditions.

3. Level-Up & Maintenance:

  • When cumulative valid bets reach the threshold, your VIP level will automatically increase the next day (no skipping levels).
  • Upon promotion/demotion, the system will recalculate valid bet amounts.
  • VIP status must be maintained by meeting the required valid bet amount within 90 days, or the level will be downgraded.

4. Level-Up Bonus: A one-time bonus will be automatically issued on the day you level up, once all conditions are met.

5. Rebate Rate Eligibility: If you reach a new VIP level and had at least NT$5 million in valid bets the day before, you’ll receive the rebate the next day based on your new level’s rate.

Level-Up Bonus Policy

1. Upon reaching a new VIP level, members will receive a one-time Level-Up Bonus, automatically issued between 12:00 and 18:00 on the same day.

2. Each VIP level bonus can only be claimed once and cannot be repeated.

3. The bonus amount is based on the new VIP level achieved — higher tiers receive greater rewards.

4. No application is required; the system will automatically distribute the bonus once conditions are met.

5. The platform reserves the right to interpret, modify, suspend, or terminate this promotion at any time.

Birthday Bonus Policy

1. All valid members are eligible to receive a birthday bonus during their birthday month.

2. The bonus will be automatically credited after login and simple verification. Each member may claim this bonus once per year.

3. The bonus amount varies based on your VIP level — the higher your tier, the greater the birthday reward.

4. The birthday bonus must be used within 7 days of receipt; expired bonuses will not be reissued.

5. The platform reserves the right to interpret, modify, pause, or terminate this promotion at any time.

Sign-in Bonus Policy

1. Members can receive a sign-in bonus by logging in and checking in daily.

2. Bonus amounts increase with consecutive sign-in days, and special rewards are granted for streaks.

3. If a day is missed, the sign-in streak will reset to day one.

4. No application is required — the bonus will be automatically credited to the member's account.

Weekly / Monthly Red Envelope & Rescue Bonus Policy

1. Weekly / Monthly Red Envelope Bonus:

  • Members who meet the required criteria (e.g. valid bets, login streaks) will receive red envelope rewards.
  • Bonuses will be sent to the member's in-site inbox, and must be manually claimed within the valid period.

2. Rescue Bonus:

  • Members who incur eligible losses within the specified time frame may receive a one-time rescue bonus.
  • Bonuses will be delivered to the inbox and must be claimed manually. Withdrawal is subject to turnover requirements.
  • Each member can claim the rescue bonus once per week only.

3. All inbox rewards must be claimed and used within 7 days of being sent, or they will expire automatically.

4. The platform reserves the right to interpret, modify, suspend, or terminate this promotion at any time.
